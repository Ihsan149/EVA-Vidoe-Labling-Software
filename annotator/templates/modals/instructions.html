Instructions

  • Class label can be selected from the drop down menu.
  • Draw a bounding box around object(s) of interest.
  • You can manually adjust existing bounding boxes.
  • To visualize press space or or use S and A.

  • Press T or Track to start the tracker.
  • The tracker runs only forward in time not affecting past annotations.
  • Press L or Lock to lock/unlock objects, locked objects are ignored by the tracker.

  • Press D or to delete a bounding box in the current frame.
  • Press Delete or to delete bounding boxes from the current frame and onwards.

  • Sequences are annotated in chunks of 100 frames, use previous and next to move between chunks.
  • Press or to move to the start or end of the current chunk.
  • Once you are done with annotation, you can export the labels (and images) from the home page .
  • Annotations are automatically saved when you move between chunks. You can manually save using Save.
